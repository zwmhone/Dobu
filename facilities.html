Premium Facilities

Our gym is equipped with everything you need for a complete martial arts and fitness experience

Matted Martial Arts Area

Our spacious matted area provides the perfect training environment for all martial arts disciplines. With high-quality shock-absorbent mats, you can train safely and effectively. The area is regularly cleaned and maintained to ensure optimal hygiene.

POPULAR

Fully-equipped Gym

Our state-of-the-art gym features a comprehensive range of cardio and strength training equipment. From free weights to resistance machines, we have everything you need to complement your martial arts training and achieve your fitness goals.

Sauna

Relax and recover in our traditional sauna. The dry heat helps to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote overall well-being. A perfect way to unwind after an intense training session.

Steam Room

Our steam room offers a moist heat experience that helps to open pores, cleanse the skin, and soothe sore muscles. The humid environment is perfect for respiratory health and post-workout recovery.

Changing & Shower Room

Our clean and spacious changing facilities include secure lockers, private showers, and vanity areas. We maintain high standards of cleanliness to ensure a comfortable experience for all members.

NEW

Relaxation Lounge

Unwind in our comfortable relaxation lounge, equipped with comfortable seating, refreshments, and a calm atmosphere. It's the perfect space to socialize with fellow members or simply relax before or after your workout.

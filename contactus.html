Frequently Asked Question


What types of martial arts classes do you offer?

DoBu Martial Arts provides classes in a variety of disciplines, including Judo, Jiu-jitsu, Karate, and Muay Thai. We also have fitness training and self-defense sessions, focusing on both physical conditioning and practical techniques. Each class is structured to cater to all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned practitioners.

Do I need any prior experience to join a class?

No prior experience is necessary. Our classes are designed to welcome everyone, regardless of skill level. Our instructors work closely with beginners to build a strong foundation and adjust the intensity for advanced students to ensure they’re always challenged and improving.

What should I bring to my first class?

For your first session, we recommend bringing comfortable athletic wear, water, and a towel. You won’t need martial arts-specific gear immediately, but if you already own items like gloves or a gi, feel free to bring them. We also have gear available at the gym if needed.

Is there a minimum age requirement for joining classes?

Yes, we offer classes for students aged 5 and up. We divide classes by age and skill level, with dedicated sessions for kids, teens, and adults. This ensures each group trains in an environment tailored to their developmental needs and abilities.

Do you offer competition training or support for athletes?

Yes, we offer specialized training for students who are interested in competing in martial arts tournaments. Our experienced instructors provide focused coaching to help athletes prepare for competitions, including strategy, technique refinement, and mental preparation.

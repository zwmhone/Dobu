Our Story

The journey of DoBU Martial Arts from a small community space to a premier training center

Our Story

DoBu Martial Arts was founded with a passion for martial arts and a commitment to empowering individuals of all ages and skill levels. Starting as a small community space, we've grown into a premier training center, offering a variety of martial arts, fitness, and self-defense classes. Our journey is built on a dedication to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment where everyone can train, grow, and connect with others who share their passion.

Our Mission

Our commitment to excellence in martial arts training and personal development

Our Mission

At DoBu Martial Arts, our mission is to inspire, educate, and strengthen individuals through martial arts. We aim to promote discipline, self-confidence, and respect in each member, fostering both physical and personal growth. By offering top-quality instruction and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to be a trusted space where members can pursue fitness, learn valuable skills, and become part of a supportive community.

Meet Our Founders

The passionate individuals who built DoBU Martial Arts from the ground up

FOUNDER

Mauricio Gomez

Gym Owner/Head Martial Arts Coach

Mauricio Gomez founded DoBu Martial Arts with a vision to create a world-class training environment accessible to all. As Head Martial Arts Coach, Mauricio brings a wealth of expertise, holding a 4th Dan Blackbelt in Judo, a 3rd Dan Blackbelt in Jiu-jitsu, and a 1st Dan Blackbelt in Karate. He is also an accredited Muay Thai coach, skilled in various martial arts disciplines. Mauricio's deep commitment to technique and personal growth inspires members to reach their highest potential.

Guy Victory

Assistant Martial Arts Coach

Guy Victory serves as the Assistant Martial Arts Coach, dedicated to supporting and training members in their martial arts journey. With a 2nd Dan Blackbelt in Jiu-jitsu and a 1st Dan Blackbelt in Judo, Guy brings both experience and passion to his role. Known for his hands-on approach and commitment to discipline, he works alongside Mauricio to ensure a supportive and enriching training experience for all members.
